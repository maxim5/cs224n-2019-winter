Office Hours

  • Time and location: are listed in the calendar at the bottom of this page.
  • Access: The exterior doors of the Gates building lock at 5:30pm, so for evening office hours please remember to bring your Stanford ID so that you can access Gates basement.
  • Attending remotely: If you're unable to attend office hours in-person (e.g. remote SCPD student), then please join the QueueStatus. One of the TAs at office hours will send you a video chat link.
  • Attending in-person: If you're attending office hours in-person, there is no need to use QueueStatus. TAs will be managing the queue with a whiteboard.
  • Staff information: Each staff member's schedule and areas of expertise are listed in the staff information section below.
